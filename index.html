To the Seven People Who Changed My Life 💜

London • 2026
Dear BTS,

It all started when I randomly watched your video.
I instantly fell in love with the warmth of your smiles.
I just wanted to know your names,
Now the rest is history.

They say everything happens at the right time,
And you came into my life exactly when I needed you.
Suddenly, I was no longer alone crying in my room,
You were there through your music and your words.

I used to hate myself and carry so much sadness,
But you taught me how to love myself, slowly and gently.
You taught me how to smile again,
And to keep moving forward, one step at a time.

Back home in India, I never thought I would have the opportunity
To see you in person.
The girl who watched your videos from her room
Could never have imagined she would one day be standing in London,
Seeing you with her own eyes.

That you would be closer to me than a screen,
That your smiles would no longer be pixels,
But real moments shared in the same place and time.

Thank you for giving me the strength to never give up.
Your music, your journey, and your words
Have encouraged me through every challenge,
Reminding me to keep trying, no matter what.

No matter how many times I say thank you,
It will never be enough.
But today, standing here,
I hope you know how much you mean to me.

Thank you for saving my life.

With all my love,
Srishti 💜
If you have scanned this page, thank you for taking a moment to read the words I wish I could say to BTS in person.
